## Industrial Engineering

Industrial engineering is a discipline focused on improving business processes and operational performance. Rather than designing products, Industrial Engineers design and improve systems—such as supply chains, logistics networks, manufacturing operations, service processes, and business workflows. Operations Research adds the analytical side, using statistics, mathematical modeling, optimization, simulation, and forecasting to support better decisions.
